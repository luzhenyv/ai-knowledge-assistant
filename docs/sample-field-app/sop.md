# Acme Field App SOP

A fictional sample SOP used to demonstrate the Knowledge Assistant. The Acme Field
App is a made-up tablet app for site inspections. The section structure mirrors a
typical procedural SOP (Install → Authorization → Select site → Checklist → Visit →
Item detail → Finding) so the assistant's retrieval, citations, and image anchoring
can be exercised end-to-end. **Nothing here is real or confidential.**

## 1. Install

Install the Acme Field App from the company app store before your first inspection.

1. Open the Acme App Store on your tablet.
2. Search for **Acme Field** and tap **Install**.
3. Wait for the download to finish, then tap the Acme Field icon to open it.

![Acme Field install screen](images/install.png)

If the app does not appear, confirm your device is enrolled and contact the helpdesk.

## 2. Authorization

The first time you open the app you must sign in and authorize the device.

1. Enter your company email and password on the sign-in screen.
2. Approve the multi-factor authentication (MFA) prompt on your phone.
3. Accept the data-handling notice to authorize the device for inspections.

![Acme Field sign-in screen](images/authorization.png)

Authorization is per-device. If you switch tablets you must authorize again.

## 3. Select site and product line

Before inspecting, choose the site and product line you are reviewing.

1. On the home screen tap **Select Site**.
2. Pick the site from the list (use search for long lists).
3. Choose the **product line** to load the matching inspection template.

![Selecting site and product line](images/select-site.png)

The selected site and product line determine which checklist items appear.

## 4. Checklist

The checklist holds every item you must inspect for the selected product line.

1. Review the generated checklist of inspection items.
2. Tap an item to see its requirements and acceptance criteria.
3. Mark each item as **Pass**, **Fail**, or **N/A** as you inspect.

![Inspection checklist view](images/checklist.png)

You can save a partially completed checklist and resume it later.

## 5. Visit

A visit groups everything captured during one on-site inspection session.

1. Tap **Start Visit** to begin a new session.
2. Work through the checklist, attaching photos where required.
3. Tap **End Visit** when finished to lock the session for review.

![Starting and conducting a visit](images/visit.png)

All checklist results, photos, and findings are stored under the active visit.

## 6. Item detail

Each checklist item has a detail screen for evidence and notes.

1. Tap an item to open its detail screen.
2. Add a photo, measurement, or comment as evidence.
3. Set the result and save to return to the checklist.

![Item detail screen](images/item-detail.png)

Evidence attached here is what reviewers see when validating the inspection.

## 7. Finding

Record a finding when an item fails or needs follow-up.

1. On a failed item tap **Add Finding**.
2. Describe the issue, set severity, and attach supporting photos.
3. Assign an owner and due date, then save the finding.

![Recording a finding](images/finding.png)

Findings are tracked to closure; the site owner is notified automatically.
